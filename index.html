What planning with AuroKey actually feels like

AuroKey doesn’t ask you to plan your trip. It asks a few questions—then does the thinking for you.

1) You tell AuroKey what matters

Trip length, who’s going, what you care about this time—quiet or energy, food or views, one base or moving.

2) AuroKey evaluates real options

It weighs destinations, timing, logistics, and constraints—then narrows the field without overwhelming you.

3) You get a Decision Summary

One recommended option, a small number of viable alternatives, and tradeoffs explained in plain language.

4) You move forward—confidently

No reopening ten tabs. No second-guessing. Just a decision that fits.

Decision Summary, the way it should be

AuroKey doesn’t just recommend. It shows the reasoning, highlights the risks, and gives you the shortest path to “yes.”

  • One top recommendation with clear “why this fits”
  • Alternatives you can actually consider
  • Tradeoffs surfaced early (not buried later)
  • Memory that compounds across trips

Start broad (“spring break, 7 days”) or specific (“quiet, food-first, no redeyes”). AuroKey adapts either way.
