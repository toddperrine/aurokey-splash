What AuroKey Is

AuroKey is not a booking site. It is a Personal Travel OS designed to help people navigate complex travel decisions.

When trips involve multiple destinations, competing preferences, real constraints, and unclear tradeoffs, AuroKey acts as a decision engine, not just a planner.

How It Works

AuroKey continuously evaluates your travel options using your preferences, your constraints, and real-world availability.

  • Recommends viable options
  • Explains why they make sense
  • Adapts as conditions change

This intelligence runs continuously—not just when you search.

Why This Is Different

Most travel platforms optimize transactions. AuroKey optimizes decision quality.

  • Memory: Persistent understanding of how you travel
  • Preference learning: Evolving patterns, not static profiles
  • Constraint resolution: Tradeoffs handled explicitly
  • Tradeoff explanation: Transparent reasoning you can trust

No existing platform owns this layer.
