Short version

AuroKey turns a messy set of preferences into a small number of great options—then shows you why.

  • Remembers what you liked and what you never want again
  • Resolves constraints (dates, budget, pace, people) explicitly
  • Explains the decision with honest tradeoffs

What “Personal” means

AuroKey gets better over time because it remembers. It builds a living understanding of how you like to travel—pace, comfort, food, neighborhoods, airlines, budget, “musts,” and “never-agains.”

So you don’t have to start from scratch every trip.

What “Intelligence” means

AuroKey doesn’t just show you options. It helps you decide. It produces a small number of strong choices and makes the tradeoffs clear—so you can move forward confidently.

In other words: fewer choices, better reasoning, less regret.

Why this matters

Most travel tools optimize for inventory and transactions. AuroKey optimizes for decision quality—powered by memory, preference learning, and clear tradeoffs.
