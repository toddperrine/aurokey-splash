How it works

A clear process for making the right decision

AuroKey is built to reduce the hardest part of travel planning: deciding. It narrows the field, explains tradeoffs, and helps you move forward confidently.

1) Preferences, not profiles

AuroKey focuses on what matters for this trip—then learns what tends to matter across trips.

  • Quiet vs energy
  • Food-first vs view-first
  • One base vs moving
  • Constraints like timing and flight tolerance
Capture what matters. Then refine over time.

3) Memory that compounds

Most tools reset every time. AuroKey improves as you use it.

  • Improves future decisions with less effort

The goal is simple: travel should feel easier the more you do it.
