What “Personal” Means

AuroKey gets better over time because it remembers. It builds a living understanding of how you like to travel—pace, comfort, food, neighborhoods, airlines, budget, “musts,” and “never-agains.”

So you don’t have to start from scratch every trip.

What “Intelligence” Means

AuroKey doesn’t just show you options. It helps you decide.

It produces a small number of strong choices and makes the tradeoffs clear— so you can move forward confidently.

What Using AuroKey Looks Like

  1. You describe the trip in your own words.
  2. AuroKey returns a few options that fit you.
  3. Each option includes what you’re choosing and what you’re giving up.
  4. You choose. AuroKey learns. The next trip gets easier.

What You Get

  • Less effort — far less planning work on your side
  • Better decisions — options shaped around what you value
  • More travel — fewer blockers between “we should go” and “we’re going”
  • More confidence — clear tradeoffs, less second-guessing
